
# 📊 Deep-Dive Business Analysis & Executive Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🎯 Project Objective
To perform a structured deep-dive analysis and build an interactive executive dashboard addressing complex business problems related to revenue growth, profitability, and performance segmentation.

This project demonstrates the ability to:
- Define meaningful business KPIs  
- Apply advanced time intelligence  
- Perform profitability deep-dive analysis  
- Translate data into actionable insights  
- Design executive-level dashboards  

---

## 🏢 Business Context
A global retail organization required a leadership-level performance overview to:

- Monitor revenue and profit growth  
- Identify key profit drivers  
- Detect underperforming product segments  
- Analyze regional contribution  
- Track Year-over-Year (YoY) trends  
- Enable data-driven decision-making  

---

## 📌 Key KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|-----|------------|
| **Total Revenue** | Measures overall business scale and growth |
| **Total Profit** | Indicates operational efficiency |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Evaluates customer purchasing behavior |
| **Total Orders** | Tracks transaction volume |
| **Total Customers** | Measures customer reach |
| **YoY Growth %** | Indicates business expansion and sustainability |

---

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis

### Focus Area: Profitability & Performance Segmentation

The analysis identifies:
- Category-wise profit contribution  
- Region-wise performance trends  
- Loss-making sub-categories  
- Growth acceleration patterns  
- Performance risk areas  

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Revenue showed strong growth, peaking in 2013  
- Technology category emerged as the top profit driver  
- Central region contributed the highest profitability  
- Tables sub-category consistently generated losses  
- Overall growth trend remained positive and stable

## 🏗 Data Modeling & Implementation
